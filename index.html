1. Click LMB on empty place to call 'create file' button, and click it to create file or move mouse from button to cancel
  2. Click RMB on file to call context menu with 'rename' and 'delete' buttons, and click on the required button or move mouse from menu to cancel
  3. You can sort files position with mouse dragging
  4. Click LMB on empty place and hold it, then move over the items what you want to select, after selecting all required files stop holding the button and click remove button to remove selected files or move mouse from button to cancel
